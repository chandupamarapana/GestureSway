Welcome to GestureSway

Are you having trouble with booking your physiotherapy sessions or are you bored with your physiotherapy sessions, Now dont you worry , we have the best solution for you. Introducing GestureSway, Where you can play a simple snake game and improve your hand mobility without going to the physiotherapy sessions , All you have to do is register into the application and play the snake game using your hand gestures. Not only you can improve your hand mobility , but also you can make your hand rehabilitation journey a fun one by playing a game .
